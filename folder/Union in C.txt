<div>What is a union?</div><div><br></div><div>Answer :-</div><div>---------</div><div>C Union is also like structure, i.e. collection of different data types which are grouped together and each element in a union is called member.</div><div>Unions and structure are same in concepts, except allocating memory for their members.</div><div>Structure allocates storage space for all its members separately.</div><div>Whereas, Union allocates one common storage space for all its members and that memory is allocated equal to that of its largest DATATYPE.</div><div><br></div>
